I love that Twitter came out with bookmarks! My pevious MO on Twitter was to “like” posts that contain information that I want to keep for later, most of which are #rstats tweets. I now have TODO likes, which is time consuming to browse through. In this post, I use the rtweet package to build a shiny app for browsing my favorites. You can use it to browse yours, too! This was my first time using the rtweet package, so I’ll also provide a brief intro. Click here to jump to the summary.
